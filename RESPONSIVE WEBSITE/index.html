Munnar

Munnar, the famed hill station of south India, is a romantic locale where natural beauty is everywhere to visit, explore and to enjoy. Munnar is situated at the confluence of three mountain streams Muthirapuzha, Nallathanni and Kundala and the word Munnar means three rivers in Malayalam.

Mysore palace

Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace, is a historical palace and a royal residence (house). It is very big palace It is located in Mysore, Karnataka, India. It used to be the official residence of the Wadiyar dynasty and the seat of the Kingdom of Mysore.

Jog Fall

Jog Falls is a waterfall on the Sharavati river located in Siddapura taluk, Uttara Kannada District and it's view point located in Shimoga district of Karnataka, India.
